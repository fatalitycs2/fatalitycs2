- ❌ **Injection Times Out**
  This usually happens when Counter Strike 2 hasn't fully loaded. Wait until you reach the main menu before clicking **Inject** in the **Fatality CS2 Menu** loader. Also confirm that no other overlay software is blocking the injection process.
